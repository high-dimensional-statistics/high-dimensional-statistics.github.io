In [Exercise 3.13]({% post_url 2020-10-10-exercise-3.13 %}), we established that
\begin{equation}
    \TV(\P, \Q) = \sup_{f \colon \X \to [0, 1]} \int f(p - q) \isd \mu.
\end{equation}
Therefore,
\begin{equation}
    2\TV(\P, \Q)
    = \sup_{f \colon \X \to [-1, 1]} \int f(p - q) \isd \mu
    = \sup_{\norm{f}_\infty \le 1} \int f(p - q) \isd \mu.
\end{equation}
